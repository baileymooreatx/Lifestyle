# Dental Care

## [Remove Plaque](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=666NgzT3gl8)  
See [coconut oil pulling dangers and benefits](https://www.drberg.com/blog/coconut-oil-pulling-dangers)  

Tea Tree Oil  4 drops
Xylitol       1 tsp
Coconut Oil   3 tbsp

Swish 1 tsp in your mouth for 10 minutes before rinsing.

## [Teeth Whitening](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=S8kPjMd8akI)  
Baking Soda           1 tsp
Hydrogen Peroxide 3%  3 Drops
Water                 8 drops

Brush with mixture for one or two minutes before rinsing.
