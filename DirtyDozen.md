# Dirty Dozen  

Produce that is most contaminated with pesticides.  
Buy organic, if possible.  

1. Strawberries
2. Spinach
3. Kale, Collards, and Mustard Greens
4. Nectarines
5. Apples
6. Grapes
7. Cherries
8. Peaches
9. Pears
10. Bell and hot peppers
11. Celery
12. Tomatoes 

## [Nontoxic Vegetable Wash](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GKLZpquBc_4)  
Mix 1/4 cup (2 fluid ounces) of 3% **Food Grade** hydrogen peroxide with one gallon of water.
Soak vegetables for 20 minutes.
