# Brewing Tea  

| Tea Type                          | Amount               | Steeping Time  | Water Temperature | Notes                                                                                    |
|:----------------------------------|:---------------------|:---------------|:------------------|:-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| [Black Tea](BlackTea.md) | 1 tsp per cup        | 3 to 5 min     | 200 to 212° F     | Best with milk or [lemon](../Fruits/Lemon.md), high caffeine                              |
| Green Tea                         | 1 tsp per cup        | 1 to 3 min     | 160 to 180° F     | Don’t use boiling water                                                                  |
| Herbal Tea                        | 1 to 1.5 tsp per cup | 5 to 7 min     | 208 to 212° F     | No caffeine, bold flavors                                                                |
| [Matcha](Matcha.md)      | 1 tsp per cup        | Not applicable | 140 to 170° F     | Don’t use boiling water                                                                  |
| Oolong Tea                        | 1 tsp per cup        | 3 to 5 min     | 185 to 205° F     | Can be resteeped, medium caffeine                                                        |
| Pu-erh Tea                        | 1 tsp per cup        | 2 to 4 min     | 200 to 212° F     | Rinse first, resteep often                                                               |
| Purple Tea                        | 1 tsp per cup        | 3 to 4 min     | 175 to 195° F     | Mild, earthy tea. Naturally low in caffeine and known for its distinct color and flavor. |
| Rooibos Tea                       | 1 tsp per cup        | 5 to 6 min     | 208 to 212° F     | Naturally sweet, no bitterness, and caffeine-free                                        |
| White Tea                         | 1 to 2 tsp per cup   | 4 to 5 min     | 170 to 185° F     | Soft taste, more delicate teas                                                           |
