# [Dr. Berg’s Daily Reboot Protocol](https://www.drberg.com/lp/daily-body-reboot-checklist/daily-routine)  

"This daily routine keeps me feeling 18 in my 60s."  

| **Time**                                              | **Tasks**                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
|:------------------------------------------------------|:------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Wake and prime phase** <br/> 6:00 a.m. to 7:00 a.m. | Wake naturally. <br/> Spend 20 minutes in sunlight. <br/> Drink 16 to 20 oz (470 to 600 mL) of water with a pinch of sea salt. <br/> Optional: Coffee with cream                                                                                      |
| **Fat-burning phase** <br/> 7:00 a.m. to 12.00 p.m.   | Remain in a fasting state. <br/> Incorporate light movement or walking.                                                                                                                                                                               |
| **First Meal** <br/> 12.00 p.m. to 1:30 p.m.          | Eat 8 to 9 oz (220 to 260 g) of high-quality animal protein. <br/> Pair with a large leafy green salad or low-carb vegetables. <br/> Include healthy fats such as olive oil, butter, avocado, or cheese                                               |
| **Energy phase** <br/> 1:30 p.m. to 5:00 p.m.         | 30 to 60 minutes of walking in nature daily <br/> Add high-intensity interval training (HIIT) or weight lifting twice a week.                                                                                                                         |
| **Second Meal** <br/> 5:00 p.m. to 7:00 p.m.          | Eat 8 to 9 oz (220 to 260 g) of high-quality animal protein. <br/> Pair with a large leafy green salad or low-carb vegetables. <br/> Include healthy fats such as olive oil, butter, avocado, or cheese. <br/> Finish eating 3 to 4 hours before bed. |
| **Wind-down phase** <br/> 7:00 p.m. to 10:00 p.m.     | Dim lights after sunset. <br/> Turn off screens 90 minutes before bed. <br/> Take 325 to 650 mg of magnesium glycinate                                                                                                                                |  
| **Sleep and rejuvenation phase** <br/> 10:00 p.m.     | Keep the room at 64 to 68°F (18 to 20°C). <br/> Ensure bedroom is dark and use white noise if needed.                                                                                                                                                 |
