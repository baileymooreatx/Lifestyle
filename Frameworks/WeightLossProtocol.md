# Weight Loss Daily Protocol

## Goal

Support healthy insulin and blood sugar levels, curb cravings, and regulate 
hunger hormones.    

## Core rules  

* Avoid snacking.  
* Limit carbs to 50 grams per day.  
* Take a walk after meals.  
* Eat an early dinner.
