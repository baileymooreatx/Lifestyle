# Weight Loss Daily Protocol  
**Goal:** Support healthy insulin and blood sugar levels, curb cravings, and regulate hunger hormones.  

**Core rules:**
* Avoid snacking  
* Limit carbs to 50 g per day  
* Take a walk after meals  
* Eat an early dinner.

## 6:00 a.m. to 7:00 a.m. &nbsp;&nbsp; Morning
Drink water with Electrolyte Powder to reduce false hunger signals  
Optional: Coffee with one scoop of Multi Collagen Peptides

## 7:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m. &nbsp;&nbsp; Fat-burning phase
Stay fasted to keep insulin low and enable maximum fat-burning  
Take a long walk  
Avoid artificial sweeteners sucralose and aspartame  
Monk fruit, stevia, xylitol, and erythritol do not trigger insulin

## 12:00 p.m. to 1:30 p.m. &nbsp;&nbsp; Meal #1
Eat 6 to 8 oz (170 to 225 g) of animal protein such as eggs, salmon, sardines, or beef
Pair with a large leafy green salad
Include healthy fats, such as olive oil, butter, cheese, or avocado
Have ½ cup L. reuteri yogurt
Walk for 15 to 30 minutes walk after eating to lower insulin without activating stress
Optional: Drink diluted apple cider vinegar or lemon water before the meal

## 1:30 p.m. to 5:00 p.m. &nbsp;&nbsp; Afternoon
Focus on high-intensity interval training (HIIT) or brisk walking
Incorporate strength workouts two to three times per week
Take 1 capsule of Natural Vitamin B1+ to support cellular energy production
Optional: For cravings, take ½ scoop of Electrolyte Powder or Magnesium Powder

## 5:00 p.m. to 7:00 p.m. &nbsp;&nbsp; Meal #2
Eat 6 to 8 oz (170 to 225 g) of high-quality protein
Pair with a large salad or non-starchy vegetables
Include healthy fats, such as olive oil, butter, and avocado
Avoid snacking after meals
Walk for 15 to 30 minutes after eating to lower insulin without activating stress

## 7:00 p.m. to 10:00 p.m. &nbsp;&nbsp; Evening
Take Magnesium Powder to support healthy cortisol balance
Turn off screens 90 minutes before bed

## 10:00 p.m. &nbsp;&nbsp; Sleep and rejuvenation phase
* Aim for 7 to 8 hours of restorative sleep
* Keep bedroom dark, quiet, and between 64 to 68°F (18 to 20°C)
