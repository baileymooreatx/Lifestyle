# Weight Loss Daily Protocol

**Goal:** Support healthy insulin and blood sugar levels, curb cravings, and
regulate hunger hormones.  

**Core rules:**

* Avoid snacking.  
* Limit carbs to 50 grams per day.  
* Take a walk after meals.  
* Eat an early dinner.  

## 6:00 a.m. to 7:00 a.m. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Morning

Drink water with Electrolyte Powder to reduce false hunger signals.  

**Optional:** [Coffee](../Drinks/Coffee.md) with one scoop of Multi Collagen
Peptides

## 7:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m. &nbsp;&nbsp; Fat-burning phase

* Stay fasted to keep insulin low and enable maximum fat-burning  
* Take a long walk .
* Avoid artificial sweeteners sucralose and aspartame.    
  Monk fruit, stevia, xylitol, and erythritol do not trigger insulin.  

## 12:00 p.m. to 1:30 p.m. &nbsp;&nbsp; First Meal  

* Eat 6 to 8 oz (170 to 225 grams) of animal protein such as eggs, salmon,
* sardines, or beef.  
* Pair with a large leafy green salad.  
* Include healthy fats, such as [olive oil](../Fats/ExtraVirginOliveOil.md),
* butter, cheese, or avocado.  
* Have ½ cup [L. reuteri yogurt](../Yogurt.md).  
* Walk for 15 to 30 minutes walk after eating to lower insulin without activating
* stress.

**Optional:** Drink diluted [apple cider vinegar](../OtherFoods/AppleCiderVinegar.md)
or [lemon](../Fruits/Lemon.md) water before the 15 to 20 minutes before the 
meal.

## 1:30 p.m. to 5:00 p.m. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Afternoon

* Focus on high-intensity interval training (HIIT) or brisk walking.  
* Incorporate strength workouts two to three times per week.  
* Take 1 capsule of Natural Vitamin B1+ to support cellular energy production.

**Optional:** For cravings, take ½ scoop of Electrolyte Powder or Magnesium Powder.

## 5:00 p.m. to 7:00 p.m. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Second Meal  

* Eat 6 to 8 oz (170 to 225 grams) of high-quality protein.  
* Pair with a large salad or non-starchy vegetables.  
* Include healthy fats, such as [olive oil](../Fats/ExtraVirginOliveOil.md), 
* butter, and avocado.  
* Avoid snacking after meals.  
* Walk for 15 to 30 minutes after eating to lower insulin without activating
* stress.  

## 7:00 p.m. to 10:00 p.m. &nbsp;&nbsp; Evening

* Take Magnesium to support healthy cortisol balance.
* Turn off screens 90 minutes before bed.

## 10:00 p.m. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Sleep and rejuvenation phase

* Aim for 7 to 8 hours of restorative sleep.  
* Keep bedroom dark, quiet, and between 64 to 68°F (18 to 20°C).
