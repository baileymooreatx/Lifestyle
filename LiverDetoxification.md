4. Choline

   * Seafood
5. Berberine
6. Coenzyme Q10 (Co-Q10) decreases AST, GGT, and high-sensitivity C-reactive protein (hs-CRP)
7. Vitamin E at 800IU daily increases rate of improvement from non-alcoholic 
   [steatohepatitis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Steatohepatitis) (fatty liver
